About Akanakintama

Hello! I' am Ange AKANAKINTAMA, a young Rwandan enthusiastic fashion blogger based in Kigali. Here on the blog I want to showcase my personality and also share the knowledge I have on Fashion, Lifestyle and Travels that will be focusing majorly on fashion, blogging and other life style topics with an aim to connect with, inspire and help people to create their best life (style and blog)

What Inspired me

Blogging has given me an outlet to just be myself, and it turns out I kind of like what am doing.I think for most of us, life can be hard and there’s a lot of figuring out to be done, a lot of conclusions to reach. Whenever I reach a rare moment of realization, I’m inclined to share it with people through writing I have an incredibly difficult time sharing my about with other people especially when am sad. And I desperately needed a way to digest my sorrow to feel as a free spirit for a certain moment Blogging has given me an outlet to just be myself, and it turns out I kind of feel free after writing down what I had in mind and some other people kind of feel connected too and learn from my experience

How it Started

My blog, began over one year ago as a solution for collating and documenting two of my greatest passions – Fashion lifestyle and Photography (actually here I mean posing, taking pictures). I’ve been a photoholic from a young age, and that became intertwined with my love of fashion and travelling (and eating), impulsively taking my time for photo shoots to complete my writings about what I met and where I went is my favorite thing.
